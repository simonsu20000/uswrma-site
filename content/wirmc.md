## About the Competition

WIRMC is our flagship event — an annual piano competition that brings together gifted pianists from around the world. Inspired by the legacy of Sergei Rachmaninoff, the competition celebrates expressive depth, technical mastery, and bold interpretation.

Each year, we welcome a diverse panel of internationally acclaimed judges and offer participants:

- Solo performance opportunities at prestigious venues
- Professional feedback and mentorship
- Access to a global community of peers and mentors
