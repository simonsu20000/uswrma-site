## Our Vision

The Washington Rachmaninoff Musicians Association was founded to discover outstanding musical talent and connect them with world-class opportunities.

As a nonprofit, non-charitable organization, we champion professionalism, discipline, and mentorship in shaping the next generation of classical musicians.

We believe music is both an art and a craft — one that requires passion, precision, and purpose.
