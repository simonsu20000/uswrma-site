About Us

Our Vision#

The Washington Rachmaninoff Musicians Association was founded to discover outstanding musical talent and connect them with world-class opportunities.

As a nonprofit, non-charitable organization, we champion professionalism, discipline, and mentorship in shaping the next generation of classical musicians.

We believe music is both an art and a craft — one that requires passion, precision, and purpose.

WIRMC Competition

Host the Washington International Rachmaninoff Music Competition — bringing together outstanding young performers on an international stage.

Education & Training

Provide masterclasses, workshops, and performance programs to develop the next generation of classical musicians.

Global Connections

Build bridges across the international music community, connecting talent with mentors and performance opportunities worldwide.
