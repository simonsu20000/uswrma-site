About the Competition#

WIRMC is our flagship event — an annual piano competition that brings together gifted pianists from around the world. Inspired by the legacy of Sergei Rachmaninoff, the competition celebrates expressive depth, technical mastery, and bold interpretation.

Each year, we welcome a diverse panel of internationally acclaimed judges and offer participants:

  • Solo performance opportunities at prestigious venues
  • Professional feedback and mentorship
  • Access to a global community of peers and mentors

Junior Division

For young emerging talents beginning their journey in classical piano performance.

Youth Division

Challenging repertoire for developing pianists ready to demonstrate their growing artistry.

Young Artist Division

An advanced platform for pianists approaching professional-level performance.

Professional Division

The highest tier — showcasing world-class technique, interpretation, and stage presence.

The 2025 Global Grand Final#

Held in New York City, USA

The Washington International Rachmaninoff Music Competition & Summer Camp Global Final was held successfully in 2025, bringing together outstanding young performers from across the globe in the world’s music capital.
